# ShellFlow

ShellFlow is a modern productivity and social application designed to help you organize your day and connect with others. It combines powerful task management with social features to keep you motivated and on track.

### 1. Task Management (Shells)
Organize your day with "Shells" - our unique take on tasks.
- **Create Detailed Tasks**: Add titles, descriptions, dates, start times, and durations.
- **Smart Organization**: Tasks are automatically sorted by time and date.
- **Daily & Weekly Views**: Easily switch between viewing your tasks for today or the entire week.
- **Quick Actions**: Rapidly add new tasks directly from your dashboard.

### 2. Social & Profile
Connect with other users and share your progress.
- **Public Profiles**: Share your profile with a unique username link (e.g., `/@username`).
- **Follow System**: Follow friends and colleagues to see their public activity.
- **Privacy Controls**: Your tasks and activity are only visible to people who follow you.
- **Follower/Following Lists**: See who's following you and who you're following.

### 3. Dashboard
Your central command center.
- **Overview**: Get a quick snapshot of your day's progress.
- **Recent Tasks**: See your most recently created tasks.
- **Quick Actions**: Fast access to common actions like creating a task.

### 4. Secure Authentication
- **Google Sign-In**: Quick and secure access using your Google account.
- **Password Protection**: Option to link a password to your account for alternative sign-in methods.

### Creating a Task
1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click the **"Add New Task"** button in the Quick Actions section.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: What do you need to do?
   - **Date & Time**: When should you start?
   - **Duration**: How long will it take?
4. Click **Create Task**. Your new shell is now ready!

### Managing Your Profile
1. Navigate to the **Profile** page.
2. Here you can see your **Daily** and **Weekly** shells.
3. Check your **Followers** and **Following** tabs to manage your connections.
4. Share your public profile link so others can follow you.

### Setting Up a Password
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Look for the **Security** section.
3. Click **"Set Password"**.
4. Enter a secure password. You can now sign in with either Google or your email/password combination.
